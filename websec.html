Web Security and YOU!

Here are some sites to help stay safe while online and surfing the web.


NetSmartz' Workshop


The NetSmartz Workshop teaches kids and their parents about staying safe online. There are tips on the basics of internet safety, and great discussion starters for parents to speak to their kids about the internet. They even talk about the cyberbullying trend that's going around.


LINK: NetSmartz' Workshop


Stay Safe Online.org


The stay safe online site has some great online safety habits advice and other such tips. They also have some online safety posters you can print out so you can raise awareness about your online safety.


LINK: StaySafeOnline.org


SafeKids.com


This site has some tips for mobile devices and what's going on in the news. It's more like a blog with posts every once in a while. Some of the posts are about social media and teens and what to do about sexting.


LINK: SAFEKIDS.COM
